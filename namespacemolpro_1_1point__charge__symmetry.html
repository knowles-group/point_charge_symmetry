Compute a cooordinate-frame-independent measure of difference between two molecules.

For each pair of atoms, we calculate for each molecule f0(rij) = 1-exp(-rij)(1+rij(1+rij/3))), which is rij*rij/6 for small rij, but tends asymptotically to 1 for large rij. This has the effect of avoiding overweighting of deviations in contributions between distant atoms. The measure is then sum_{i>j} (f0(rij,A)-f0(rij,B))^2 / (n*(n-1)/2), which is bounded by 0,1
